C.1 Simple Harmonic Motion

Definition

Simple harmonic motion (SHM) occurs when the restoring force is directly proportional to displacement from equilibrium position.

Key Equations

F = -kx

x = A cos(ωt)

v = -Aω sin(ωt)

a = -Aω² cos(ωt)

T = 2π√(m/k)

Where:

A = amplitude

ω = angular frequency

T = period

Energy in SHM

E = ½kA² = ½mv²max

KE = ½mv² = ½kA² sin²(ωt)

PE = ½kx² = ½kA² cos²(ωt)
