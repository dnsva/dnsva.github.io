E.2 Quantum Physics

Wave-Particle Duality

Light and matter exhibit both wave and particle properties, known as wave-particle duality.

Key Equations

E = hf

λ = h/p

Where:

h = Planck's constant (6.63 × 10⁻³⁴ J·s)

f = frequency

λ = wavelength

p = momentum

Photoelectric Effect

Figure 1: The photoelectric effect showing electron emission

Einstein's Photoelectric Equation

hf = Φ + KEmax

Where:

Φ = work function

KEmax = maximum kinetic energy of ejected electrons
