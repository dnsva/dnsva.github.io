B.3 Gas Laws

Ideal Gas Law

PV = nRT

Where:

P = pressure (Pa)

V = volume (m³)

n = number of moles

R = gas constant (8.31 J/mol·K)

T = temperature (K)

Special Cases

Boyle's Law: P₁V₁ = P₂V₂ (constant T)

Charles's Law: V₁/T₁ = V₂/T₂ (constant P)

Gay-Lussac's Law: P₁/T₁ = P₂/T₂ (constant V)
