A.1 Kinematics

Key Concepts

Kinematics describes motion without considering its causes.

Essential Equations

v = u + at

s = ut + ½at²

v² = u² + 2as

Where:

v = final velocity

u = initial velocity

a = acceleration

t = time

s = displacement
