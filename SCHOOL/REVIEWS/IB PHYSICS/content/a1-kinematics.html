A.1 Kinematics

Basic Concepts

Kinematics is the study of motion. It involves several key quantities:

• Displacement (Δx): The change in position of an object

• Velocity (v): The rate of change of displacement

• Acceleration (a): The rate of change of velocity

Key Equations

Average velocity: $v_{avg} = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t}$

Instantaneous velocity: $v = \frac{dx}{dt}$

Acceleration: $a = \frac{dv}{dt}$
