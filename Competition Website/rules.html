TBPC RULES

Only one computer per team
Only paper material is allowed (no googling or online documentation)
Scrap paper is allowed
Calculators are allowed but not necessarily needed
Any programming language allowed
Output must match exactly (do not add prompts)
For every wrong answer you SUBMIT, 20 minutes are added to your total time

Teams are first ranked by the number of questions solved and then by time. So, if two teams solve all five questions, first place will go to the team who took the least amount of time.
